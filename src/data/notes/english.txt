LBS MCA ENTRANCE EXAM 2025 - ENGLISH QUESTIONS

Q101. Communication through television is __ communication.
A) Interpersonal
B) Public
C) Mass
D) Mass Media
Answer: D
Explanation: Television reaches a large audience simultaneously, making it Mass Media communication.

Q102. __ is the agency of central government that gives information regarding government policies and projects.
A) PIB
B) PTI
C) AIR
D) PCI
Answer: A
Explanation: PIB (Press Information Bureau) is the nodal agency of the Government of India to disseminate information about its policies and programmes.

Q103. __ is not a social network.
A) LinkedIn
B) Instagram
C) Twitter
D) Ebay
Answer: D
Explanation: Ebay is an e-commerce platform, not a social network. LinkedIn, Instagram, and Twitter are social networks.

Q104. They __ here for 10 years.
A) are living
B) have been living
C) has been living
D) has lived
Answer: B
Explanation: For an action that started in the past and continues to the present, use present perfect continuous: have been living.

Q105. He __ Tennis every day.
A) play
B) is playing
C) has played
D) plays
Answer: D
Explanation: For habitual or routine actions, use simple present tense: plays.

Q106. She discussed it __ others.
A) from
B) to
C) with
D) among
Answer: C
Explanation: The correct preposition with discuss is with. She discussed it with others.

Q107. They reached Guruvayoor __ 12 pm.
A) at
B) on
C) from
D) to
Answer: A
Explanation: The preposition at is used with specific times. They reached at 12 pm.

Q108. He __ for a movie yesterday.
A) goes
B) go
C) went
D) gone
Answer: C
Explanation: Yesterday indicates past tense. The simple past of go is went.

Q109. When I reached the railway station, the train __ already.
A) left
B) had left
C) has left
D) is leaving
Answer: B
Explanation: When one past action happened before another past action, use past perfect (had left) for the earlier action.

Q110. He is smarter than __ boys in the class.
A) many other
B) any other
C) no other
D) none of these
Answer: B
Explanation: In comparative degree with than, use any other: smarter than any other boy.

Q111. Principal as well as the faculty members __ present.
A) are
B) were
C) have
D) is
Answer: A
Explanation: When as well as joins two subjects, the verb agrees with the first subject. Principal (singular) + as well as... = are (plural context treated as plural here since faculty members follows).

Q112. Fire force __ the fire to avoid huge loss.
A) put up
B) put out
C) put up with
D) put on
Answer: B
Explanation: Put out means to extinguish. Fire force put out the fire to avoid huge loss.

Q113. Don't argue __ that mad lady.
A) from
B) at
C) against
D) with
Answer: C
Explanation: Argue against means to oppose or contend. Don't argue against that mad lady.

Q114. __ are the pillars of communication.
Options given: 1. listening and speaking, 2. listening and helping, 3. helping and speaking, 4. reading and writing
A) 1 and 2 only
B) 2 and 3 only
C) 1 and 4 only
D) 3 and 4 only
Answer: C
Explanation: The four pillars/skills of communication are listening, speaking, reading, and writing. Options 1 (listening & speaking) and 4 (reading & writing) together cover all four.

Q115. He sits __ his friend and his wife.
A) on
B) among
C) at
D) between
Answer: D
Explanation: Between is used when referring to exactly two people or things. Between his friend and his wife.
