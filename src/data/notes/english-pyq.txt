LBS MCA ENTRANCE EXAM - ENGLISH PYQ (2025)
================================================================

Q. Communication through television is _____ communication.
A) Interpersonal
B) Public
C) Mass
D) Mass Media
Answer: D
Explanation: Television reaches a large, diverse audience simultaneously, making it Mass Media communication.

Q. _____ is the agency of central government that gives information regarding government policies and projects.
A) PIB
B) PTI
C) AIR
D) PCI
Answer: A
Explanation: PIB (Press Information Bureau) is the central government agency that disseminates information about government policies and projects.

Q. _____ is NOT a social network.
A) Linkedin
B) Instagram
C) Twitter
D) Ebay
Answer: D
Explanation: Ebay is an e-commerce platform, not a social network. LinkedIn, Instagram, and Twitter are social networks.

Q. They _____ here for 10 years.
A) are living
B) have been living
C) has been living
D) has lived
Answer: B
Explanation: For an action that started in the past and continues to the present, use present perfect continuous: have been living.

Q. He _____ Tennis every day.
A) play
B) is playing
C) has played
D) plays
Answer: D
Explanation: For habitual or regular actions, use simple present tense. He plays (third person singular).

Q. She discussed it _____ others.
A) from
B) to
C) with
D) among
Answer: C
Explanation: The correct preposition after discuss is with. You discuss something with someone.

Q. They reached Guruvayoor _____ 12 pm.
A) at
B) on
C) from
D) to
Answer: A
Explanation: The preposition at is used with specific times. They reached at 12 pm.

Q. He _____ for a movie yesterday.
A) goes
B) go
C) went
D) gone
Answer: C
Explanation: Yesterday indicates simple past tense. The simple past of go is went.

Q. When I reached the railway station, the train _____ already.
A) left
B) had left
C) has left
D) is leaving
Answer: B
Explanation: When one past action happened before another past action, use past perfect (had left) for the earlier action.

Q. He is smarter than _____ boys in the class.
A) many other
B) any other
C) no other
D) none of these
Answer: B
Explanation: In comparative superlative context with than, use any other. He is smarter than any other boy means he is the smartest.

Q. Principal as well as the faculty members _____ present.
A) are
B) were
C) have
D) is
Answer: D
Explanation: When two subjects are connected by "as well as", the verb agrees with the first subject, not with the subject that follows.

Q. Fire force _____ the fire to avoid huge loss.
A) put up
B) put out
C) put up with
D) put on
Answer: B
Explanation: Put out means to extinguish. Fire force put out the fire is correct.

Q. Don't argue _____ that mad lady.
A) from
B) at
C) against
D) with
Answer: D
Explanation: We use argue with a person when we mean having a disagreement or quarrel with them.

Q. _____ are the pillars of communication. (1. listening and speaking, 2. listening and helping, 3. helping and speaking, 4. reading and writing)
A) 1 and 2 only
B) 2 and 3 only
C) 1 and 4 only
D) 3 and 4 only
Answer: C
Explanation: The four pillars of communication are listening, speaking, reading, and writing. Options 1 and 4 together cover all four.

Q. He sits _____ his friend and his wife.
A) on
B) among
C) at
D) between
Answer: D
Explanation: Between is used when referring to two people or things. He sits between his friend and his wife (two people).
